Features

1) Reminders
You can set your habits straight from the reminders to begin your routine. At any time, date, interval, you can have unlimited access and flexibility in how you manage your habits.

You can share your progress, completed tasks with your friends all on real-time, and even view others on the "active feed." Your well on your way to becoming a better person!

Features

2) Calendar
Track your habits every month right through the calendar feature. Besides tracking your habit schedule, you can also use the calendar to make plans and events so that you can stay consistent!

Features

3) Online Forums
Stay in touch with the community! Share what's on your mind and inspire. View success stories from others. View challenges that others have. Almost anything that you post here can be shared and viewed by others. Have fun and be social!
